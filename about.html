About Me

My name is Derrek Do, I'm a first generation vietnamese american student at North Seattle Colleges. I am currently studying computer science and working towards a Bachelors degree.
Originally I had been studying physics in hopes to major in astronomy but made the swap after some thinking. In 2021 I took a beginner CS course as an elective, I enjoyed it and decided to complete the subsequent courses. Even though I found it just as difficult as physics, I had more fun to learn it and decided to pivot over to CS

What I look forward most to AD320, is that I will be able to make web based applications or sites comfortably by the end of the quarter.
